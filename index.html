The space

The studio features a generous live room with a PA system, a diverse range of amplifiers, an 8-piece drum kit, keyboards of various types and some speciality instruments such as a wind organ.

In the separate live room, we have a Logic-based recording setup featuring an Allen & Heath mixer and a PreSonus Quantum audio interface. We are currently capable of recording up to 8 channels concurrently, and the community centre has a fast Internet connection, perfect for livestreaming directly from the studio.
